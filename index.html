How to Lose 委員會組織章程

Charter of the How to Lose Committee

第一條 基本資訊

Article 1: Basic Information

  • 一、本委員會為How to Lose委員會
  • 二、在本章程之範圍內：
    • 1.被告係指遭舉發而待爭議裁決委員會審理之人
    • 2.「字樣」係指任何文字或其組合、句子、或其他圖畫、聲音、影像
  • 1.This committee shall be the How to Lose Committee
  • 2.In this charter--
    • (1) "defendant" means a person who has informed against and awaiting decisions by the Dispute Resolution Committee
    • (2) "characters" means any words, or their combinations, or sentences, or graphical or audio contents

第二條 委員

Article 2: Members

本章程所指之委員以委員名冊為準。委員名冊以群組成員為準。

The members referred to in this charter are based on the member list. The member list is based on the group members.

第三條 爭議裁決委員會

Article 3: Dispute Resolution Committee

  • 一、設立爭議裁決委員會，許委員為當然委員，審理爭議事項。當然委員因故不能視事時，由其餘三人一同充任之
  • 二、有下列以下情勢，當然委員應自行迴避：
    • 1.當然委員為嫌疑者
  • 三、有違反How to lose 組織精神之言論，均得提交爭議裁決委員會審理
  • 四、經爭議裁決委員會全數委員同意有罪，有罪判決始成立
  • 1. There shall be a Dispute Resolution Committee, with Commissioner Xu as the ex officio member, to handle disputes. If the ex officio member is unable to serve, the remaining three members shall serve together.
  • 2. The ex officio member shall find themselves recused, in the scenario that--
    • 1. The ex officio member is a suspect
  • 3. Any speech that violates the spirit of the How to Lose organization may be submitted to the Dispute Resolution Committee for review.
  • 4. A guilty verdict is established only with the unanimous consent of all members of the Dispute Resolution Committee.

第四條 罰鍰管理委員會

Article 4: Fine Management Committee

  • 一、設立罰鍰管理委員會，洪委員為當然委員，綜理罰鍰之處分，然應符合組織利益
  • 二、犯本章程者，未經爭議裁決委員會之有罪判決確定，不得裁罰
  • 1. There shall be a body of committeeman , with Commissioner Hong as the ex officio member, to handle fines, and all punitive fines shall be in the interest of this committee.
  • 2. Those who violate this charter shall not be fined without a guilty verdict confirmed by the Dispute Resolution Committee.

第五條 禁止事項

Article 5: Prohibited Actions

  • 一、不得故意抬舉本委員會會之成員或自我貶抑，違者處10元罰鍰，得按次罰鍰
  • 二、不得惡意發佈包含「⚡️」字樣之訊息或包含「電」、「卷」字樣之訊息，違者處10元罰鍰，得按次罰鍰
  • 三、不得惡意發佈包含連續二以上「⚡️」字樣之訊息或以「🛐」字樣接續前項之字樣，違者處30元罰鍰，得按次罰鍰
  • 四、不得惡意發布英文之字樣，或其他包含英文之圖畫、影像、聲音
  • 1. Members of this committee shall not intentionally praise the members of this committee or belittle themselves, and violators shall be subject to a punitive fine of 10 dollars, which may be fined per violation.
  • 2. Malicious messages containing the word "⚡️" or messages containing the words "電" or "卷" is prohibited, and violators shall be fined 10 dollars, which may be fined per violation.
  • 3. Malicious messages containing two or more consecutive "⚡️" characters or following the previous characters with one or more "🛐" characters is prohibited, and violators shall be fined 30 dollars, which may be fined per violation.
